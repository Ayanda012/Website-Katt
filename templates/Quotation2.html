Rate

Cost on average ranges between R60 000 and over R250 000.

Cost is determined by:

  • Solar Size (in kW)
  • System Type
  • Invertor Type
  • Installation Complexity

Off-Grid: More expensive due to the cost of batteries and additional components required for energy storage. Cost ranges between R150 000 and R350 000

5kW solar system ranges between R70 000 and R140 000. Note a 10kW system produces on average 40-50kWh of power a day, enough to power two small families or one large family.
