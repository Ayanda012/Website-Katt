Rate

Cost on average ranges between R60 000 and over R250 000.

Cost is determined by:

  • Solar Size (in kW)
  • System Type
  • Invertor Type
  • Installation Complexity

Hybrid System: Combines the 2 above, i.e. connected to the grid with power stored kicking in during cloudy days & power outages. Also feeds the grid. Due to its nature, it is less costly than Off-Grid yet slightly more than grid-tied solar systems.

5kW solar system ranges between R70 000 and R140 000. Note a 10kW system produces on average 40-50kWh of power a day, enough to power two small families or one large family.
